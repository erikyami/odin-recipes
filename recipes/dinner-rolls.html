Quick Dinner Rolls Page

Homemade dinner rolls in under an hour? Absolutely! If you’re short on time or feeling slightly nervous about your skills as a baker, these quick dinner rolls are exactly what you need.

They are every bit as fluffy and tender as rolls that take all afternoon to make—so much so that I may never go back to making anything else!

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup (180 milliliters) milk (whole, 2%, or 1%)
  • 3/4 cup (180 milliliters) water
  • 2 tablespoons (25g) sugar
  • 1 tablespoon (11g) active-dry or instant yeast
  • 3 1/2 to 4 cups (420g to 480g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted or salted butter, melted, plus more for pan

Steps

  1. Dissolve the yeast
  2. Knead the dough
  3. Let the dough rise
  4. Meanwhile, preheat the oven to 400°F
  5. Shape the rolls
  6. Let the rolls rise
  7. Cool briefly and serve
